       Specific templates per D-06 and CHOREOGRAPHY-RULES.md section 6:
       - entry: A -> D -> X (3 points along centerline)
       - extension: 4 diagonal variants — H->F (toward C, strength), M->K (toward C, strength), F->H (away from C, weakness), K->M (away from C, weakness). Each diagonal has ~4 points.
       - lateral: 2 variants per side — along left long side (K->H direction) or right long side (F->M direction). ~4 points.
       - pirouette: small circle near G (y=0.15, toward C) for strength, near L (y=0.65, toward A) for weakness. ~8 points approximating circle.
       - passage: centerline from D toward C (strength) or from I toward A (weakness). ~4 points.
       - piaffe: short segment near G (strength) or near L (weakness). ~3 points.
       - skridt: along long side, ~4 points.
       - kontragalop: along long side continuing past corner, ~5 points.
       - changer: diagonal through X, ~4 points.
       - volte: circle at E or B, ~8 points for smooth circle approximation.
       - overgang: corner area, ~3 points.

    5. **selectVariant(type, rating, rng)**: Pick from variants based on StrengthRating:
       - "strength" -> prefer variants where average y < 0.3 (toward C, per D-09)
       - "weakness" -> prefer variants where average y > 0.7 or average x near 0/1 (per D-10)
       - "neutral" -> standard variant (per D-11)
       Per D-12, this is integrated into variant selection, not a separate transform.

    6. **generateRoutes(programOrder, ratings, options?)**: Per D-01 signature. Iterates programOrder, classifies each exercise, selects route variant based on rating and seed. Uses mulberry32 PRNG (copy from program-generator.ts pattern). Returns ArenaRoute[].
